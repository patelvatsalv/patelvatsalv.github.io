Vatsal V. Patel

Hello! I'm a robotics / mechanical engineering PhD candidate in the GRAB Lab at Yale University advised by Prof. Aaron M. Dollar. I work on developing robot hands that can manipulate everyday objects in complex tasks through novel mechanical designs, parallel mechanisms, motion planning, and optimization! I graduated with bachelors (w/ Dept Citation!) and masters from University of California, Berkeley. In summer 2023, I was a research intern at the Boston Dynamics AI Institute.

I have also worked at Intuitive Surgical, and taught as an ("outstanding") graduate student instructor, provided technical insights to Yale Ventures, and helped build comunity through many engineering and campus student groups!

Feel free to get in touch: v.patel[at]yale.edu.
